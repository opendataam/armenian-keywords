## Notes for Database Matching
- Some toponyms appear in multiple forms (e.g., Ереван/Эривань, Гюмри/Ленинакан/Александрополь)
- "Армавир" can refer to both a city in Russia (Krasnodar Krai) and a city in Armenia - check context
- "Карабах" and "Нагорный Карабах" are historically Armenian regions
- "Закавказье" (Transcaucasia) includes Armenia but also Georgia and Azerbaijan - check for Armenian-specific context
- Historical names (Эривань, Александрополь) should match modern equivalents (Ереван, Гюмри)
- Regional names like "Сюникская обл." or "Арагацотнская обл." are strong indicators
- Terms like "Армянская ССР" or "Армения" combined with any location name indicate Armenian context
