## Important Notes:
# 1. "Армавир" appears in both Russian (Krasnodar Krai) and Armenian contexts - verify with region/province
# 2. Historical names should match: Эривань=Ереван, Александрополь=Ленинакан=Гюмри, Кировакан=Ванадзор
# 3. "Закавказье" alone is not sufficient - must be combined with Armenian-specific location
# 4. Case-insensitive matching recommended for Latin script (Armenia, Yerevan)
# 5. Consider fuzzy matching for common misspellings (Армения vs Арменипя, etc.)
